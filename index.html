About Me

Independent Petroleum Engineering Technologist focused on upstream production optimization and field-proven innovation.

From Concept to Field — Building Production Technology for West Africa

I am an independent Petroleum Engineering Technologist with a singular mission: to solve the production challenges that keep Niger Delta marginal fields from reaching their full potential. My work spans the entire production optimization stack — from downhole mechanical solutions to surface gas-lift systems, AI-native artificial lift platforms, and autonomous reservoir intelligence probes.

My innovations are designed specifically for the mature, marginal, and stranded assets that dominate the West African landscape. Each technology is field-realistic, cost-conscious, and engineered for indigenous fabrication and deployment.

Downhole Hardware

Niger Delta Survivor

A slimline tubing anchor catcher engineered for the unique challenges of Niger Delta marginal fields. Reduces tubing movement, prevents packer failure, and extends well life in mature assets. Designed for indigenous fabrication with embedded sensor readiness for the ND-Amahor platform.

Surface System

ND-GLO

Containerized solar-hybrid wellhead gas-lift optimizer that eliminates flaring while boosting production. Features auto-bypass logic, H2S cartridge treatment, and autonomous operation without SCADA dependency. The surface complement to the Niger Delta Survivor.
